Description
Castle Craft is a RPG top down fantansy game. Your job is protecting the castle from the enemies. To protect the castle, you might need to collect the resources and craft weapons from the collected resources to fight the enemy. There are 5 different waves, each waves will have different number of enemies and different types of enemies. Remember, the final boss at the last wave is very strong, you should consider build towers to able to defeat him. Otherwise, you will lose.

Team Reflection
In the beginning, our team has 2 people, but after 2 weeks the team splits because my teammate doesn't work on the project and always delay my work. To make the game deliver on time with features planned, I spend a lot of time to work on my own. Working on the project by myself, it doesn't make myself depressed, instead, it elevates my passion for game development. You able to see my game improments through playtests and final delivery.
Future Improments
